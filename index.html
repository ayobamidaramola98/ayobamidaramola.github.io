About Me

I am a dedicated Modelling & Simulation Engineer with expertise in computational modelling, numerical simulation, and machine learning integration for complex engineering challenges. My research spans energy systems, material behavior, and multiphase dynamics. I am passionate about innovation in energy transition technologies and digital twins.

Research Work

Atomistic Simulation of Structural Defects in Austenitic High Entropy Alloys

Developed simulation tools for multiscale modelling using Python, C++, and Fortran. Investigated structural defects in high entropy alloys.

Numerical Modelling of Reactor Materials

Led research on thermomechanical behavior and heat distribution using Python-based tools and CFD software.

Hydrogen Networks & Extreme Environment Materials

Ongoing research at the University of Edinburgh focusing on multiphase simulations and materials in extreme conditions.

Machine Learning Integration in Simulation

Leveraged gradient boosting, neural networks, and support vector regression to enhance simulation accuracy and predict failure risks.
